💬 General

What is APR vs interest rate?
The interest rate is the cost of borrowing the principal. APR includes the rate plus fees, giving a truer picture of annual cost.
How do I lower my monthly payment?
Choose a longer term, secure a lower rate, or borrow less. Longer terms reduce monthly payments but increase total interest.
Can I pay off my loan early?
Most personal loans allow early repayment. Some lenders charge a prepayment penalty. Extra principal payments reduce total interest significantly.
What credit score do I need?
Most lenders require 580–600 minimum. Best rates go to borrowers above 720–740.
Is this calculator accurate?
This uses the standard amortization formula with a fixed rate and no fees. Confirm exact figures with your lender.
What is an amortization schedule?
A table showing every payment over the loan's life — principal vs interest each month.

🏠 Mortgage

What is a good mortgage rate?
Current 30-year fixed rates range from 6–7.5%. A credit score above 740 qualifies for the best rates. Compare at least 3 lenders on the same day.
How much house can I afford?
Housing payment (PITI) should not exceed 28% of gross monthly income. For $6,000/month income, that's $1,680/month max.
15 vs 30 year mortgage?
15-year has higher payments but costs ~half the interest. $300k at 6.5%: 30-year = $382k interest, 15-year = $169k interest.

🚗 Car loans

What is a good car loan rate?
750+ credit: 4–6%. Good credit (690–749): 6–9%. Below 660: often 10–15%. Credit unions generally beat dealership financing.
Should I put money down on a car?
10–20% down is recommended. It reduces monthly payments and lowers risk of being underwater as cars depreciate quickly.

🔄 Refinancing

When should I refinance?
Consider when rates drop 1%+ below your current rate, your credit score improves significantly, or you want to switch from ARM to fixed.
How much does refinancing cost?
Closing costs typically run 2–5% of the loan, or $4,000–$10,000 on a $200,000 loan. Use the break-even calculator to decide.
